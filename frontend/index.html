How It Works

Simple. Transparent. Automated.

From listing to return — every step is tracked on the platform with no manual paperwork.

01

Create an Account

Register as an owner, a renter, or both. Your profile tracks all activity in one dashboard.

02

Browse or List

Owners list equipment with photos, pricing, and availability. Renters search and filter by category.

03

Book & Confirm

Submit a booking request. Terms are locked in — fees, deposit, and duration are crystal clear.

04

Deposit Held

A security deposit is tracked automatically. No manual chasing, no disputes over amounts.

05

Track & Return

Due dates and overdue flags are handled by the system with clear status updates throughout.

06

Release & Review

On return, the deposit is released and the transaction closes cleanly — logged on your history.

Problem Statement

Why T0kenRent needs to exist

Informal equipment rental is broken. Here's what the platform fixes.

🗂️

No Centralized Platform

Listings, availability, and user records are scattered across chats and papers — no single place to manage them.

📝

Informal Agreements

Verbal or handwritten contracts lead to misunderstandings over fees, duration, and item condition.

⚙️

Zero Automation

Due dates, deposits, and late penalties are all tracked manually — the biggest source of disputes between parties.

🔒

Lack of Trust

Without a structured system, both owners and renters hesitate to engage in peer-to-peer equipment sharing.
