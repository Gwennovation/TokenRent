⚡ Step-by-Step Guide

How T0kenRent Works

From signing up to releasing your deposit — see exactly how every rental flows through the platform, end to end.

01
Start Here

Connect Your Wallet or Create an Account

Connect your HandCash wallet for a frictionless blockchain-based identity, or create a free email account. Your profile tracks everything you rent in one dashboard.

  • HandCash users: one-click sign-in with your $handle
  • Email users: standard registration with secure password
  • No fees to join — you only pay when you book
02
Discover

Browse & Search the Marketplace

Filter equipment by category, location, or availability. Each listing shows clear photos, the daily rate, security deposit, owner profile, and reviews from past renters.

  • Live filters: Construction · Photography · Outdoor · Events · Vehicles · Electronics
  • Status badges show whether an item is Available, Rented, or Overdue
  • Sort by price, popularity, or newest listings
03
Book

Request a Rental

Pick your dates, see the total cost broken down (rental fee + platform fee + refundable deposit), and submit your booking request. The owner reviews and confirms.

  • Date picker prevents conflicts with existing bookings
  • You see the exact total — no hidden fees
  • You're not charged until the owner accepts
04
Pay & Pick Up

Secure Payment & Item Handover

When the owner accepts, your payment is processed and the security deposit is held in escrow. Coordinate pickup details directly through the platform's messaging.

  • Deposit is held safely — not paid out to the owner
  • Item handover confirmed by both parties in-app
  • Smart contract logs the rental period and condition
05
Use & Return

Rent the Item, Then Return It

Use the equipment for your agreed rental period. Return on time and in the same condition you received it.

  • Reminders sent before your due date
  • Need an extension? Request one in-app, the owner can accept or decline
  • Late returns trigger overdue fees as agreed in the listing
06
Wrap Up

Get Your Deposit Back & Leave a Review

On safe return, your security deposit is automatically released back to your wallet or card. Leave a review for the owner — it helps everyone in the community.

  • Deposit released within minutes of return confirmation
  • Transaction is permanently logged in your history
  • Reviews build trust for both sides of the marketplace
01
Get Set Up

Connect a Wallet & Verify Your Identity

Sign in with HandCash or create an email account. Verified owners get a trust badge and rank higher in search results.

02
Create Your Listing

List Your Equipment in 3 Minutes

Upload photos, write a clear description, choose a category, and set your daily rate plus security deposit. You can also set availability windows.

  • Multi-step form with progress tracking
  • Drag-and-drop photo upload (up to 6 photos per item)
  • Save drafts and finish later
03
Receive Requests

Review & Approve Booking Requests

Renters submit booking requests. You see who they are, their rental history, and reviews from other owners before deciding.

  • Accept or decline with one click
  • Message the renter directly before approving
  • You're never auto-booked — every rental needs your green light
04
Hand Off

Hand Over the Item

Coordinate pickup with the renter through in-app messaging. Both sides confirm the item's condition at handover.

05
Track

Monitor Active Rentals

Your dashboard shows every active rental, upcoming returns, and any overdue items. Send reminders automatically when something is late.

06
Get Paid

Receive Payment & Build Your Reputation

When the item is safely returned, your rental fee is paid out and the renter's deposit is released. Build a track record to attract more renters.

  • Platform fee: 5% — that's it
  • Payouts straight to your HandCash wallet or bank
  • Every successful rental boosts your rating

Common Questions

Frequently asked

What is HandCash and why do I need it?
HandCash is a Bitcoin SV wallet that lets you sign in with a $handle instead of a password. Your rental history, deposits, and payouts are tied to your wallet — making everything transparent and portable. If you'd rather use email, that works too.
How is my security deposit kept safe?
The deposit is held in escrow — neither you nor the owner can access it during the rental. It's only released when both parties confirm the item was returned in good condition. If there's a damage dispute, the platform helps mediate.
What if the item gets damaged?
Minor wear-and-tear is expected. For real damage, the owner files a claim with photos. Both parties present their case, and the platform's dispute resolution releases the appropriate portion of the deposit. We keep all messages and condition photos on record.
How much does it cost to list an item?
Listing is completely free. T0kenRent only takes a 5% platform fee on completed rentals — that's it. No monthly fees, no listing fees, no hidden charges.
Can I cancel a booking?
Yes. Renters can cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before the rental starts. Owners can cancel any time before handover. Late cancellations may incur a small fee, set by the owner in the listing.
Is T0kenRent only for the Philippines?
For this initial phase, T0kenRent is focused on the Philippines (₱ pricing, local pickup). International expansion is on the roadmap once we've validated the model locally.
